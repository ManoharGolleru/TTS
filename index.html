Higgs TTS 3 Conversation Lab

A guided playground for testing conversational speech behaviors: turn holding, repair, backchannels, question contours, affect, timing, pauses, and sound effects. It uses Higgs inline tags, not SSML.

Workflow run-through

Choose a taskPick a conversational phenomenon, not just a voice.
Write the turnDraft the utterance and include the transcript cue you want tested.
Add controlsApply leading tags at the start, insert pauses and sound effects at exact locations.
ValidateCheck tags, stream settings, text length, and SSML leftovers.
Listen and annotateCapture analyst notes for timing, affect, intelligibility, and failure cases.
